How do I use the trade assistant?

The trade assistant works by taking either a CSGO Lounge Trade URL or a steam profile URL to either show all the important statistics for the items in the trade or the items in said person's inventory. When entering trades the tool will also immediately show if the submitted trade is a good or bad trade. Another feature is the ability to paste CSGO Lounge Profile URLs right into the text field. The tool will automatically fetch the user's steam profile and get the appropriate data for his items.

New!: You can now click or click and drag across prices to have the site automatically sum price selections of your choice. This can be useful when looking at someones inventory as it allows you to quickly calculate a fair trade out of the items available.
